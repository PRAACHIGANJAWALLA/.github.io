About me

I'm Praachi, a Business Analysis enthusiast based in Boston, MA. I am a strategically driven Analyst with unwavering determination and a resilient spirit. Fueled by curiosity and innovation, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in data analysis, financial operations, and business process optimization. With a keen eye for detail and a creative spark, I craft solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations, delivering impactful results that leave a lasting impression. I earned a Master's in Business Analysis with a focus on Financial Analysis from the University of Massachusetts, Lowell. Prior to that, I gained a Bachelor's degree in Accounting & Finance from the University of Mumbai. My professional journey includes successful roles as an Admissions Data Analyst at UMass Lowell, where I have successfully implemented data analysis using Tableau, SQL and Salesforce. I've worked as a Data Analyst Intern at Digital Ferderal Credit Union (DCU) where I successfully completed a capstone project and built a Statistical Model to predict loss for the credit union. Prior to that, I've worked as a Financial Analyst at Die Plast Pvt Ltd, where I managed financial data, financial statements and used tools like SAP ERP and Power BI. I'm dedicated to data-driven decision-making, innovation, and excellence, and I'm eager to apply my skills and experience to new challenges in financial and business analysis.
